About Us

We help drivers save time, reduce stress, and move more freely by making parking smarter and easier.

We're here to make parking as simple as possible.

Our goal is simple: to provide clear, accurate, and real-time information about parking slots for the Technological University of the Philippines - Manila, so everyone can enjoy a smoother driving experience.

Not just for experienced drivers, but for everyone. We offer a reliable and convenient alternative to the usual stress of circling around looking for a space.

We don't think parking should be about frustration or guesswork. With our system, every slot is updated in real-time so you can head straight to where you need to go. Parking becomes less about waiting, and more about moving forward.

Meet Our Members!

We are Computer Science students from Technological University of the Philippines - Manila dedicated to creating innovative solutions that address real-world problems. This parking availability system is part of our academic journey and aims to help drivers easily locate available slots, reduce traffic congestion, and support parking personnel in managing spaces more efficiently. Through this project, we strive to apply our knowledge and skills in computer science to build a practical, user-friendly system that makes parking smarter and more convenient for everyone.
